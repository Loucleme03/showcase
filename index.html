Demo 3 - Esri-Leaflet Map

This demonstration uses Esri Leaflet Maps where it displays 2 raster images of the exact same clipping extent to show the difference in the landscape between 1984 and 2021. The raster images were sourced from USGS. To see the difference you can slide the slider to show changes in landscape overtime
